Air BNB Dashboard

  • Developer
  • Kian

This project analyzes how location affects Airbnb pricing in New York, showing that proximity to the city, scenic views, or popular areas raises rates even when property size or amenities are similar to rural listings. I created an interactive Tableau dashboard where anyone can explore the data: locations are color-coded by price, and bedroom prices are shown for 1–6 room properties, making it easy to see pricing patterns across the city.
